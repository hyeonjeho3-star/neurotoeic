# TOEIC Essential Vocabulary
tags: vocabulary, business, TOEIC

---

Q: A formal agreement between two or more parties
A: contract (계약)

Q: To make something officially valid
A: validate (유효화하다)

Q: Money paid regularly for work
A: salary (급여)

Q: A meeting to discuss business matters
A: conference (회의)

Q: To send goods to another country
A: export (수출하다)

Q: The date by which something must be completed
A: deadline (마감일)

Q: A person who buys goods or services
A: customer (고객)

Q: To officially end employment
A: terminate (해고하다)

Q: A written or printed statement of money owed
A: invoice (송장)

Q: To increase in size, number, or importance
A: expand (확장하다)
