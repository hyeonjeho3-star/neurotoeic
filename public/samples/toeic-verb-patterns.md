# TOEIC Verb Patterns
tags: grammar, verbs, TOEIC, Part5

---

The manager {{c1::agreed to meet::동의하다}} with the client tomorrow.
|trap: agreed meeting, agreed meet, agreed on meet

She {{c1::suggested going::제안하다}} to the new restaurant for lunch.
|trap: suggested to go, suggested go, suggested that going

They {{c1::finished reviewing::완료하다}} the documents before the deadline.
|trap: finished to review, finished review, finished reviewed

We {{c1::avoid making::피하다}} hasty decisions in our department.
|trap: avoid to make, avoid make, avoid made

The team {{c1::considered hiring::고려하다}} a new consultant.
|trap: considered to hire, considered hire, considered that hire

He {{c1::denied taking::부인하다}} the confidential files.
|trap: denied to take, denied take, denied that take

The company {{c1::postponed launching::연기하다}} the new product.
|trap: postponed to launch, postponed launch, postponed that launch

She {{c1::admitted making::인정하다}} a mistake in the report.
|trap: admitted to make, admitted make, admitted that make

We {{c1::recommend using::추천하다}} the new software system.
|trap: recommend to use, recommend use, recommend that use

The staff {{c1::enjoyed working::즐기다}} on the project together.
|trap: enjoyed to work, enjoyed work, enjoyed that work
